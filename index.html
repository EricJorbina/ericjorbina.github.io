🎮 Game Dev Project

From Nowhere is a narrative-driven indie game about unraveling a mysterious house that blurs the lines between memory and reality. As the protagonist wakes up from a nightmare with the tasked to find out what or why the house is calling him, players must explore dreamlike landscapes, interact with strange Entities, and solve puzzles to discover who they are and what brought them to this place. It’s a journey about self-identity, trauma, and redemption set in a surreal universe.
